Search the words, not just the labels.

deathlessons.org indexes the full text of every Prevention of Future Death (PFD) report — including scanned reports recovered with OCR and the agency responses — so you can find any drug, device, hospital, or hazard mentioned anywhere in a report. Tools that only filter by coroner area, date and category can't do that.

For researchers, journalists & organisations

The public search is free and always will be. For deeper or programmatic use we offer paid services on top of this public data:

  • Bulk dataset download — the cleaned, OCR'd, metadata-tagged corpus as JSONL/CSV.
  • Saved-query alerts — get emailed when a new PFD matches your search (e.g. a drug or device you monitor).
  • Bespoke analytical reports — we answer a specific question across the whole corpus.
