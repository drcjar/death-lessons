Search the words, not just the labels.

deathlessons.org indexes the full text of every Prevention of Future Death report — including scanned reports recovered with OCR and the agency responses — so you can find any drug, device, hospital or hazard mentioned anywhere in a report. Tools that only filter by coroner area, date and category can't do that.
